About

"The Red Fingers project means the search for the new and the breaking of certain traditions when creating music. My idea is to be able to find a new and different sound every time I work with an artist."

Red Fingers is a Chilean producer, musician and composer, based in Los Angeles, United States. He has worked with renowned artists such as Bad Bunny, Rauw Alejandro, Paloma Mami, Pablo Chill-E, among others. His collaborations and songs as a producer already have more than 150 million views on different platforms, making him one of the music producers with the greatest rise and projection in the industry.
