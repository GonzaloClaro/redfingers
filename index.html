About

"The Red Fingers project means the search for the new and the breaking of certain traditions when creating music. My idea is to be able to find a new and different sound every time I work with an artist."

Red Fingers is a Chilean producer, musician and composer based in Los Angeles. His path runs from electronic music through reggaetón and Latin urban into rap and trap, a constant search for sounds that don't exist yet. Along the way he has worked with Bad Bunny, Rauw Alejandro, Paloma Mami and Pablo Chill-E, among others, and his productions have crossed 150 million views. The numbers travel far; the search is what stays.
